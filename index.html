the City VR Experience

City VR allows you to discover new perspectives to look upon cities like never have before. Touch and feel the skyscrapers. Teleport to go places. Take snapshots of favorite views and moments. Enjoy sunset from the top of the world.
What is City VR?
City VR is an immersive virtual reality experience that allows you to discover new perspectives to look upon the world we live in like never have before. Every street and address matches its real-world equivalent - but City VR is more than just a 3D map. It enables you to travel through the streets like a giant, observe architecture from up on high, zoom in to the ground or zoom out to the air, change lighting and weather, and take snapshots of the most unique city views discovered on your own.
When and how can I get City VR?
City VR will be released on Steam on September 20th, 2016. City VR is currently available on HTC Vive, and additional HMDs are coming soon.
Who created City VR?
